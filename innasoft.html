Social Media Marketing (SMM)

SMM services

Social media services are provided according the contract. Initially we generate accounts at different social media networks according your choice and make connections according the targets. The report is sent to you with the timetable of work and followers achieved. Then on your demand we can continue this service or you can continue making the links and get followers yourself.

Step 1.

Fill in the enquiry form of 'Social media services' from InnaSoft professionals. Choose the preferred social networks: Facebook, Digg, MySpace, Twitter, Flickr, Delicious, Google, Yahoo, Reddit. If you have already registered in these social networks, we request the login and the password to publish blogs, content or promotional videos that raise awareness of your brand, service or product on your behalf. We can register your profile there as well. We issue blogs, content, promotional videos and links during week, month or 12 months as agreed.

Step 2.

In the enquiry please send us the key words we should be focused on. These keywords give a steady stream of targeted traffic of visitors who can be potentially your customers.

Step 3.

After the payment will be received, we create accounts and make links during the prepaid period.

Step 4.

This service can be provided during 10 hours / 1 month / 3 months / 1 year / long term contract.

Step 5.

Then on your demand we can continue this service or you can continue making the links and advertising of your services or product yourself via generated social networks accounts.
